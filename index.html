OUR ACHIEVEMENTS

Innovating for Efficiency: ATL Tinkering Lab's RO System Solution

In 2021, ATL Tinkering Lab students at SPS Rupnagar embarked on a project to combat water wastage from RO systems, evolving it from a classroom idea to a practical solution.


Their efforts spanned two aspects: firstly, creating an advanced system for under-construction buildings, embedding water-saving mechanisms in initial designs for sustainable practices.Secondly, they crafted a retrofitting model to equip existing structures with water-saving features for effective water conservation.


The outcomes were impressive; the project earned recognition within their local academic community and the commercial sector for its pragmatic application. This acclaim propelled their project to the national stage, where it earned a place among the top 30 models across the country. This achievement highlighted not only the project's significance but also the students' dedication to impactful water conservation strategies.
